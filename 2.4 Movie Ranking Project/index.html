My Top 3 Movies

Indiana Jones and The Last Crusade

I really think Sean Connery and Harrison Ford had great synergy in this movie.

Ninja Scrolls

The scene where Ganmon (I Believe) tears off a guys arm. The camera view pans from top down. He is holding an arm over his gaping maw, severed end down, gulping. It was fantastic.

Caddyshack

Just an all around great cast and funny movie. I could watch this on repeat and have on a day when there was nothing better to do. Ah those were the days...
